Privacy Policy

FRC TCG  |  Effective Date: March 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to FRC TCG ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our web application at frctcg.com. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use the App:

Google Account Email Address: When you sign in with Google, we collect your email address to identify your account and associate your card collection and match history with you.

Card Binder Data: We store the cards you have added to your binder and any match-related data (such as wins) in Google Sheets linked to this application.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to operate and improve the App. Specifically, we use your email address to:

  • Identify you as a user and retrieve your personal card binder.
  • Record match results, including first-win rewards.
  • Allow you to create and join multiplayer matches with other users.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties. Your data is only used within the App to provide the service described above.

5. Data Storage

Your data is stored in Google Sheets and Google Apps Script infrastructure, which are governed by Google's own privacy and security policies. We do not operate independent servers. Data is retained for as long as your account remains active or as needed to provide the service.

6. Google OAuth

This App uses Google OAuth to authenticate users. By signing in, you grant the App permission to read your Google account email address. We do not access your Google Drive, Gmail, contacts, or any other Google services beyond what is explicitly required to run this application.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with their information without parental consent, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at frctcg2720@gmail.com. We will remove your email and associated card binder and match data upon request.
