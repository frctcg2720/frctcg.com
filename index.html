WHAT IS FRC TCG?

FRC TCG is a card game featuring cards made by FRC teams that celebrate the people and robots who make FIRST great! At competitions you can collect physical cards and store them in this app's binder. Pick 3 cards and battle a friend right in the app — and win rewards for your first victory!

🃏
COLLECT
Pick up physical cards at competitions and add them to your digital binder. Each card has unique HP, abilities, and energy costs.
⚔️
BATTLE
Challenge friends to live 1v1 matches with a 6-digit code. Use abilities, manage energy, and outplay your opponent.
🏆
WIN REWARDS
Win your first match and claim a limited edition physical card from teams 2720 or 2992's pit!

HOW TO PLAY ↗

1
